# Clearledgr Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

## Overview

Clearledgr is a Chrome extension that helps finance teams execute Accounts Payable workflows directly inside Gmail. This policy explains what data we process and why.

## Data We Collect

### Email Data (Processed Locally)
- What: Subject, sender, snippet, and attachments for invoice emails you process
- Why: To extract invoice metadata and create AP items
- Where: Parsed locally in your browser. Only extracted invoice fields are sent to our servers

### AP Metadata
- What: Vendor, amount, currency, invoice number, dates, approval status, ERP reference
- Why: To execute the AP workflow and maintain an audit trail
- Where: Stored securely in the Clearledgr backend

### Operational Logs
- What: Error logs and workflow traces
- Why: Reliability and auditability
- Where: Stored securely in the backend

## Data We Do Not Collect
- Full email content beyond invoice metadata
- Passwords or credentials
- Browsing history outside Gmail

## How We Use Your Data
1. Create and update AP items
2. Route approvals in Slack
3. Post approved entries to an ERP
4. Maintain an immutable audit log

## Third Party Integrations
- Slack for approvals
- ERP connectors for posting

Each integration requires explicit authorization and can be revoked.

## Data Retention
- AP items and audit events are retained for compliance
- Retention periods can be configured per customer
